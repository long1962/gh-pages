Welcome

We are Chad and Missy Faughn owners of Copper Top BBQ. We both grew up here in Fredonia and have lived here all of our lives. For many years Chad has been making the best ribs and BBQ for family and friends and through their encouragement we decided to open Copper Top. In May of 2013 our dream was finally realized and we started selling our BBQ along with brick oven pizzas.

Now our days start mighty early with the preparation of delicous slow smoked ribs, pulled pork, brisket and chicken and our homemade salads, desserts and side dishes. We put in the hours of commitment and preparation so that the whole family can be satisfied with offerings from home style BBQ meals and "Old World Style" Brick Oven Pizza.

So if you are traveling along the Trail of Tears or just out on a beautiful drive through sunny valley Fredonia Kentucky, stop by and see us for eat-in or carry-out and maybe take home a pint of our delicious secret recipe sauce.
